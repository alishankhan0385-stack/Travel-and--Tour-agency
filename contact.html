Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to typical travel questions below

What does a standard Roamify travel package include?

Unless specified otherwise, our packages generally include verified 4-star or higher accommodations, daily breakfast, private ground airport transfers, and guided activities led by local experts.

Can I customize an itinerary on your website?

Absolutely! Every package featured on our Destinations page can be fully tailored. Just reach out via the contact form on this page with your custom requests, and a designer will craft it for you.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

We offer a 100% refund on bookings cancelled up to 14 days prior to the departure date. For cancellations within 14 days, a 50% refund or travel credit option is provided.
